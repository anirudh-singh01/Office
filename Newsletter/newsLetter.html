KI Aug 2025 Hotfix - Important Enhancements

Hot Fix 1 Custom Email Notifications

Now contains all important details including title and link.

Hot Fix 2 Comment box size

Comment box size and text-limit has been increased for all actions: Send Back To Author, Reassign, Reject and Archive.

Hot Fix 3 Complete Article Access and Edit-Privileges

All Reviewers (KM, TR, LR) assigned to the article have complete article access and edit-privileges.

a. The article detail section can be edited using "Edit" button on the top or pencil icons for respective section.

b. The Product L1/L2 can now be modified by reviewers themselves – No need to send it back to author.

Hot Fix 4 Change Level of Review and Manually Choose

Selected KMs can change level of review and manually choose TR and LR overriding system auto-assign after their approval.

Hot Fix 5 Approval Work Items

All Salesforce knowledge users (authors and reviewers) can now see article approval and rejection stages in the 'Approval Work Items' section at the end of the article page

Note: In the event where Reviewer sends an article back to author for correction, the approval work item of this article continues to show the same reviewer's name as this article will come back to the same reviewer once author resubmits an article.

Hot Fix 6 Removed "Recall" option

Removed "Recall" option altogether as it was causing confusion. Users can use 'Chatter' with @name tag or teams or email to suggest change/action to latest assignee of the article by knowing it from "Assigned To" field in the Article Approval Work Items at the end of article page as shown in above point #5.

Hot Fix 7 Re-assign

Reviewers can now reassign article to any salesforce active reviewer, and not just from the system reviewers list

Hot Fix 8 Pending Items For Your Review (New Flow)

The pending article sections "Items to Approve" under Home Page has, for the time being separate sections for both old-flow (pre SolvNetPlus Modernization - 28 June Release) and new flow articles.

Hot Fix 9 In-Author Review Visibility

The pending article sections "Items to Approve" under Home Page has, for the time being separate sections for both old-flow (pre SolvNetPlus Modernization - 28 June Release) and new flow articles.

Note: We will now not show "In-Author Review" article in above list to reviewer who sent it back to author as it is not under his action at that moment. Yes, it will come back to same reviewer.

Hot Fix 10 Mandatory Comments

Comments box at various places in the content management flow is now made mandatory. This enables reviewers to add their comments while they are performing tasks associated with article such as 'Reassign', 'Send Back to Author, 'Reject' & 'Archive'.

Note: The comment section at the time 'Approve' will be made non-mandatory soon!
