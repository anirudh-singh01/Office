KI Aug 2025 Hotfix - Important Enhancements
 
Hot Fix 1
 
Complete Article Access and Edit-Privileges

All Reviewers (KM, TR, LR) assigned to the article have complete article access and edit-privileges.

a. The article detail section can be edited using "Edit" button on the top or pencil icons for respective section.

b. The Product L1/L2 can now be modified by reviewers themselves – No need to send it back to author.
Hot Fix 2
 
Change Level of Review and Manually Choose

Selected KMs can change level of review and manually choose TR and LR overriding system auto-assign after their approval.
Hot Fix 3
 
Approval Work Items

All Salesforce knowledge users (authors and reviewers) can now see article approval and rejection stages in the 'Approval Work Items' section at the end of the article page

Note: In the event where Reviewer sends an article back to author for correction, the approval work item of this article continues to show the same reviewer's name as this article will come back to the same reviewer once author resubmits an article.
Hot Fix 4
 
Removed "Recall" option

Removed "Recall" option altogether as it was causing confusion.

Users can use 'Chatter' with @name tag or teams or email to suggest change/action to latest assignee of the article by knowing it from "Assigned To" field in the Article Approval Work Items at the end of article page as shown in above Hot Fix #5.
Hot Fix 5
 
Re-assign

Reviewers can now reassign article to any salesforce active reviewer, and not just from the system reviewers list
Hot Fix 6
 
Pending Items For Your Review (New Flow)

Under Home, author can also see articles pending under their review (In-Author Review) if a reviewer has sent it back for corrections.
